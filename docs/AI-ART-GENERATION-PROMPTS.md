## Technical Specifications

- **Image Size:** 1024x1024px for characters, 1920x1080px for scenes
- **Art Style:** Pure anime/manga style (NOT semi-realistic)
  - Characters: Shoujo/Shounen manga aesthetic, detailed anime eyes, cel shading
  - Scenes: Anime background art (Makoto Shinkai, Studio Ghibli inspired)
- **Keywords:** anime, manga, cel shading, anime eyes, bishounen, shoujo, shounen
- **Avoid:** realistic, photorealistic, 3D render, semi-realistic
- **Color Palette:** Vibrant anime colors, warm tones, soft pastels for romantic scenes
- **File Format:** PNG for characters (transparency), JPG for scenes
- **Optimization:** Compress images to < 500KB for web performance

## Recommended AI Tools

1. **Midjourney** - Use `--niji 6` mode for pure anime style
2. **NovelAI Diffusion** - Specialized for anime/manga art
3. **Stable Diffusion** with anime models:
   - Anything V5
   - CounterfeitXL
   - AnimeArtDiffusion
4. **Leonardo.ai** - Anime XL presets

## Generation Tips

- **Midjourney:** Use `--niji 6 --style expressive` for characters, `--niji 6 --style scenic` for backgrounds
- **Negative prompts:** `realistic, photorealistic, 3D, semi-realistic, western cartoon, ugly, distorted, low quality, blurry, watermark`
- **Character prompts:** Add `shoujo manga, cel shading, anime eyes with highlights, bishounen`
- **Scene prompts:** Add `makoto shinkai style, anime background art, detailed scenery`
- Generate multiple variations (4-8) and pick the best
- Ensure anime eyes are large, expressive, and have proper highlights
- Maintain consistent pure anime style across all images
- For scenes, focus on atmospheric lighting and detailed backgrounds
